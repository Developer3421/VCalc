# Privacy Policy for VCalc

**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## Overview

VCalc ("we", "our", or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and storage.

## Data Collection

**VCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.**

Specifically:
- ✅ **No user accounts** - VCalc does not require registration or login
- ✅ **No data storage** - VCalc does not save any files or calculation history
- ✅ **No internet connection** - VCalc operates entirely offline and does not connect to any servers
- ✅ **No tracking** - VCalc does not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking mechanisms
- ✅ **No third-party services** - VCalc does not integrate with any external services
- ✅ **No cookies** - VCalc does not use cookies or similar technologies

## What VCalc Does

VCalc is a purely local calculator application that:
- Performs mathematical calculations in real-time on your device
- Displays results temporarily in the application window
- Does not persist any calculation data after the application is closed

## Data Retention

**VCalc does not retain any data.** All calculations are performed in memory and are immediately discarded when:
- You clear the calculator display
- You close the application
- You restart your device

## Information Sharing

Since VCalc does not collect any data, there is no information to share with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

VCalc is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

## Your Rights

As VCalc does not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the application simply by using it on your device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of VCalc after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- **Email:** support@vcalc.app
- **Website:** https://vcalc.app

## Compliance

VCalc complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Since VCalc does not collect any data, compliance is achieved by design.

**In Summary:** VCalc respects your privacy by simply not collecting any data at all. What happens in VCalc, stays in VCalc - and only until you close the window.
